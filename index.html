How to play Where to Eat

GAME RULES:


- The game has 2 players


- Whose ever turn it is to choose where to eat is selected by pressing the change player button


- The current player is highlighted by the red dot next to their name


- To select a random restaurant the player must press the 'Choose Restaurant' button


- The game will only run once and display a random restaurant to eat at


- Once the current player as pressed the choose restaurant button the game ends


- To start a new game, press 'Change Player' which will allow the other player to then select a random restaurant


- Enjoy your meal!
