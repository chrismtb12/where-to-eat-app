How to play Pig - Dice Game

GAME RULES:


- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds


- In each turn, a player rolls a both dice as many times as they wish. Each result gets added to their ROUND score


- BUT, if one of the die rolls a 1, all their ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn


- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that their ROUND score gets added to their GLOBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn


- Players can choose to set a high score they wish to play to, if the box is empty the game will finish at a score of 100


GOOD LUCK!
